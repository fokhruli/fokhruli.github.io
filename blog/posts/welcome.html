Welcome to my blog — what I'll be writing about

Apr 12, 2025  ·  Md Fokhrul Islam  ·  meta research

This is the first post on my personal blog. I've wanted a place to write informally about things I'm thinking about — outside of the structured format of papers and presentations.

What I'll write about

I plan to use this space for a few different things:

  • Research notes — ideas I'm exploring, things that confused me, things that clicked.
  • Paper readings — short summaries and takeaways from papers I find interesting.
  • Grad school reflections — lessons learned as a first-year PhD student.
  • Misc — books, chess games, anything else worth sharing.

Why write at all?

Writing forces clarity. When I try to explain something in words, I quickly discover which parts I actually understand and which parts I've been glossing over. I hope these posts will be useful to others, but honestly they're primarily for me.

"If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough." — attributed to Einstein
