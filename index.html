Md Fokhrul Islam

PhD Student & RA · School of Data Science, UVA

UVA SDS  /  UVA CDT  /  fokhrul.rmedu@gmail.com

I am a first-year Data Science PhD student at the University of Virginia, advised by Professor Heman Shakeri. My research is at the intersection of multi-modal learning, geometric representation learning, and interpretable machine learning, with applications in healthcare, robotics, and social good.

Broadly, I am interested in three directions:

  • Representation learning on geometry — learning from graphs, skeletons, and point clouds with limited supervision.
  • Multimodal & low-resource learning — bridging vision, language, and knowledge graphs for zero-shot recognition.
  • AI for health — building automated assessment and rehabilitation systems using deep learning.

Before UVA I completed my MSc and BSc in Robotics & Mechatronics Engineering at the University of Dhaka, where I worked with Dr. Sejuti Rahman.
